## Personalization ideas (by role)

**CEO hooks:**

- Reference a recent growth move, expansion, or market shift their company is navigating
- Reference their board or investor narrative around margin or growth efficiency
- Use a quote from a recent interview or earnings call

**COO hooks:**

- Reference operational complexity: multi-site, coverage gaps, workforce headcount decisions
- Reference a supply chain challenge, nearshoring move, or automation initiative they've announced
- Reference specific KPIs they'd be responsible for (fill rate, utilization, coverage ratio)

**CFO hooks:**

- Reference pressure to tie operational decisions to documented financial outcomes
- Reference AI tools / digital investments that haven't shown clear ROI yet
- Reference difficulty aligning FP&A forecasts with operational planning teams
