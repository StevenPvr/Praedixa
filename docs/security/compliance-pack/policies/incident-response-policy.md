# Incident Response Policy (Template)

## Purpose

Define incident handling from detection to closure.

## Severity

- P1: major security/service impact
- P2: moderate impact
- P3: low impact

## Workflow

1. Detect and classify.
2. Contain and communicate.
3. Eradicate and recover.
4. Post-mortem and preventive actions.

## Requirements

- Incident register entry required.
- Timeline and root cause required.
- Customer communication defined by severity.
